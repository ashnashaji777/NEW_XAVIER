PROGRAM OVERVIEW

BCOM Corporate is an undergraduate degree program.

Why BCOM at St. Xavier's?

St. Xavier’s is a premier institute with an excellent academic track record.

DURATION

This is a 3-year full-time course.

ELIGIBILITY

Pass with 45% aggregate for 12th Standard Examinations (10+2).

ADMISSION PROCESS

Admissions are normally offered on the basis of an interview.

PROGRAM HIGHLIGHTS

  • 100% Placement
  • Business English Sessions
  • Soft Skill Training
  • Workshops and Training
  • College Fests
  • Wi-Fi Enabled Campus
  • Internships and Externships
  • Guest Talks

MAJOR SUBJECTS

  • Financial Accounting Company Law and Secretaryship Practice
  • Managerial Economics
  • Human Resource Management
  • International Trade
  • Corporate Finance
  • Business Communication
  • Marketing
  • Auditing
  • Income Tax Law and Practice
  • Elements of Insurance

SPECIALIZATIONS

1 Accounting
2 Finance
3 Banking & Insurance
4 Marketing
5 HR Human Resources
6 IT

FEE STRUCTURE

Particulars Amount
ARC 30,000/- INR
1st Year Tuition Fees 40,000/- INR
2nd Year Tuition Fees 60,000/- INR
3rd Year Tuition Fees 60,000/- INR
Total (for 3 years) 1,90,000/- INR
