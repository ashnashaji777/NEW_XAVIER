WELCOME TO ST XAVIER'S COLLEGE BANGALORE

St. Xavier's Bangalore is one of the top Business Schools under Bangalore City University. In the unremitting momentum of time, St. Xavier’s College, Bangalore has managed to achieve mainstream prominence. It has become a reconvening point for students from a plethora of backgrounds, dedicated as the College is to be a reagent for the junction of opportunities and strengths through skill development and knowledge-sharing programmes. The faculty who have proven themselves in their respective fields by providing an excellent blend of rigor and relevance in their teaching; staff who are ever ready to reach out; robust industry interactions; research aimed at solving problems of the real world; a plethora of international collaborative arrangements in the anvil; outreach activities that touch the lives of a wide cross-section of society; and an exemplary track record in career counseling placement facilitations—all combine to provide a rare synergy that transcends artificial barriers and enables students to follow their hearts with passion and confidence.

Reasons to study at ST XAVIER'S College

Equipped with years of rich legacy St Xaviers College imparts high quality, interdisciplinary education at an affordable cost. Come, be a part of one of the best institutions in Bengaluru.

World Class Faculty

Outstanding and highly qualified faculty members with an excellent curriculum framed in consultation with top academics and industry experts.

Pioneering Research

Ample opportunity for students to get involved in research under the guidance of experts. They also get to use the latest facilities and tools to discover and publish.

Global Exposure

Collaborations with top class national and international universities to create learning opportunities through transformative exchange programmes.

Programs at St Xavier's College

Be a part of our world-class education with the combination of academic and research programs, set in international standards.
