STUDENTS SECURED 10 LAKH PACKAGE

St Xavier’s Bangalore has maintained an excellent track of highest salary packages throughout the past five years. There has been a significant increase in our recruiters, including top global MNC’s, which has increased the placement statistics considerably over the past years. Along with the benefit of getting placed in top MNC’s, students are offered attractive salary packages ranging from 8 to 13 LPA, with a median average of 10 LPA. Some of the most sought-out domains are Data Science, Human Resources, Marketing, Business Analytics, Banking, and International Business. The flexible curriculum, dynamic pedagogy, dedicated placement cell, and best faculty expertise at SXB contribute majorly to this evergreen pathway of lucrative jobs with handsome packages.
